Our Story

Welcome to Yakkala Timber Merchant!

Yakkala Timber Merchant is a timber products company since 1999. We produce variety of wood products and services including flooring, staircases, doors and pantries.

Our wood products are manufactured using Mahogani, Teak, Attonia, Kubuk and Jack trees. Our priority is providing quality and satisfactory services to our customers.
